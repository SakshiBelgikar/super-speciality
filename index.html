Our Top Medical Equipments

Pulmonary Function Test (PFT)

Our PFT machine provides precise measurements of lung function, aiding in the diagnosis and management of respiratory conditions such as asthma, COPD, and other lung disorders.

X-Ray

Our advanced X-Ray technology delivers clear, high-resolution images for accurate diagnosis of fractures, infections, and various internal conditions, ensuring prompt and effective treatment.

Treadmill Test (TMT)

The TMT machine is used to evaluate cardiovascular health by monitoring heart function under stress, helping detect coronary artery disease and assess overall cardiac fitness.

2D Echocardiogram (2D Echo)

Our 2D Echo machine provides detailed images of the heart's structure and function, allowing for accurate assessment of heart conditions and guiding appropriate treatment plans.

Electrocardiogram (ECG)

The ECG machine records the heart's electrical activity, helping diagnose arrhythmias, heart attacks, and other cardiac conditions with quick and reliable results.
